Course Schedule

The following are my upcoming courses

FREC 3 course - blended
First Response Emergency Care Level 3
Book via Eventbrite

A blended 5 day course with 3 days online and 2 longer face to face days with assessments.

As a HCPC paramedic myself I bring real life experience and examples to my training. Using this blending learning approach the 2 face to face days can be mostly practical in nature.

Participants will need to be able to get down to the floor in order to perform good quality CPR, and patient care.

The course incorporates trauma care, minor injuries and illnesses, intermediate life support skills, defib use and much more.

Holders of the FREC3 qualification can go on to achieve FREC4 which is the equivalent to an Emergency Care Assistant in many NHS ambulance trusts.

Any questions do contact me including any concerns about pricing and accessibility, I will do my best to answer and will always be open and honest.
